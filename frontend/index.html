FAQ

Common
questions.

Still not sure? Here are answers to the most common questions we get about NourishAI.

Is NourishAI free to use?
Yes, completely free. Generate as many plans as you like with no subscription or credit card required.
How accurate are the calorie counts?
Our model achieves 99.9% accuracy on calorie estimation using a validated nutritional database cross-referenced with clinical dietary guidelines.
Can I use it if I have multiple health conditions?
Absolutely. NourishAI supports multi-condition filtering — select all that apply and the AI will find meals that safely satisfy all restrictions simultaneously.
What cuisines are supported?
Pakistani, Mediterranean, Chinese, Italian and Indian cuisines are fully supported, along with Vegetarian and Vegan dietary preferences.
Can I regenerate my plan?
Yes — if you don't love your plan, hit Regenerate on the results page for a completely fresh set of meals that still matches all your requirements.
Does it include exercise recommendations?
Yes. Every plan includes personalised exercise recommendations with duration, intensity and specific activity types matched to your goal and activity level.
